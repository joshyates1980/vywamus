Privacy Policy for The Vywamus Foundation

Effective Date: May 1st, 2026

The Vywamus Foundation ("us", "we", or "our") operates www.vywamusfoundation.org (the "Site"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Site.

1. Information Collection and Use

While using our Site, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address, address, and phone number ("Personal Information").

We collect this information for the purposes of providing the service, identifying and communicating with you, responding to your requests/inquiries, and improving our services.

2. Log Data and Cookies

Like many site operators, we collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Site ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your computer's IP address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our Site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and other statistics.

We do not use "cookies" to collect information but know that you can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a third-party service that collect, monitor, and analyze user information to provide reports of our Site's functionality. The third-party service provider we use is Plausible Analytics (https://plausible.io) and their team has their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

4. Data Security

The security of your Personal Information is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we collect. To exercise these rights, please contact us here.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of May 1st, 2026 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will be in effect immediately after being posted on this page.
